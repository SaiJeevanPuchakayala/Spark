COURSE OVERVIEW:
This introductory course covers the fundamental concepts of Artificial
Intelligence, including search algorithms, machine learning basics,
natural language processing, and ethical considerations in AI.

SCHEDULE:
Week 1:  What is AI? History and Foundations
Week 2:  Search Algorithms (BFS, DFS, A*)
Week 3:  Knowledge Representation
Week 4:  Machine Learning Basics
Week 5:  Supervised Learning (Classification, Regression)
Week 6:  Unsupervised Learning (Clustering, Dimensionality Reduction)
Week 7:  MIDTERM EXAM
Week 8:  Neural Networks Fundamentals
Week 9:  Natural Language Processing
Week 10: Computer Vision Basics
Week 11: Ethics in AI
Week 12: Final Project Presentations

GRADING:
- Assignments (4 total): 40%
- Midterm Exam: 20%
- Final Project: 30%
- Participation: 10%

LATE POLICY:
Assignments lose 10% per day late, up to 3 days maximum.
After 3 days, assignments receive a zero unless an extension was granted.

ACADEMIC INTEGRITY:
All submitted work must be your own. Collaboration on assignments is
encouraged for discussion, but each student must write their own code
and report. Use of AI tools (ChatGPT, etc.) must be disclosed.
